Frequently Asked Questions

Answers from our surgical team

What is Dr. Mohamed's experience in the hair transplant field?

Having gained extensive knowledge training with some of the world's most distinguished hair restoration surgeons, Dr. Mohamed is one of the few surgeons highly specialised in both advanced follicular unit extraction (FUE) and follicular unit transplantation (FUT) procedures.

What is Dr. Mohamed's specialism?

As discretion is key for Dr. Mohamed's high-profile clients, he has developed his technique significantly to provide FUE procedures with minimal downtime. He is known for creating natural results that are virtually undetectable and specialises in both FUE and FUT methods.

Which hair transplant procedures does Dr. Mohamed perform?
  • Advanced FUE techniques
  • Male, female and transgender pattern baldness
  • Afro-textured hair FUE
  • FUE correction or revision procedures
