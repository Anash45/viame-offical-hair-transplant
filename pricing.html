How our pricing works

What Is Transparent Pricing?

The Traditional Pricing Model

Per-graft charging can appear low at first but often creates an incentive to over-quote grafts and increase final costs.

Many clinics price every graft individually, which can lead to quotes that exceed what is truly needed for natural coverage.

Our Fixed Package Pricing

Official Hair Clinic uses fixed transplant size categories: small, medium, large, and mega.

Your surgeon assesses donor and recipient zones, then recommends the right package to achieve natural density without overpaying.

Pricing and Payment FAQs

What is the average cost of a hair transplant in the UK?

Hair transplant costs in the UK can vary widely depending on graft count, clinic expertise, and aftercare. Official Hair Clinic provides transparent fixed-package pricing so you know exactly what is included.

Can I get finance for my procedure?

Yes. We offer 0% financing options that allow you to spread the cost over manageable monthly payments.

How can I get an exact quote?

Your quote is confirmed after a surgeon-led consultation where we assess your hair loss pattern, donor capacity, and treatment plan.

Are aftercare and follow-up visits included?

Our packages include structured follow-up support so your recovery and growth progression are monitored properly.

Are online calculators accurate?

Calculators are useful for rough estimates, but they cannot replace a full surgeon consultation for a precise, personalised quote.
