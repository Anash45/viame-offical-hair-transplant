Understanding Hair Transplant Costs

What Determines the Price of a Hair Transplant?

1. Pricing model

Some clinics charge per graft, while others offer fixed pricing based on transplant size.

2. Aftercare and extras

Some clinics include follow-ups and PRP therapy, while others charge extra.

3. Clinic reputation and location

Prices tend to be higher in major cities like London due to demand. Some clinics charge the cost of a reputable clinic but then outsource the procedure to smaller local partner clinics to lower their costs.

4. Surgeon experience & technology

A highly skilled, reputable surgeon will cost more. Advanced FUE techniques often cost more than older methods.

5. Hidden costs

Some clinics offer low upfront prices but charge for additional consultations after consultation, medications, and follow-ups.

All our consultations are led by a doctor—not a sales person—who personally assesses your hair-transplant plan.

How Much do Post-operation PRP Sessions Cost?

PRP therapy typically costs £500 per session in the UK. 

City Clinics includes four complimentary PRP sessions in your package.

Platelet-rich plasma (PRP therapy) is a non-invasive treatment that uses your body’s growth factors to stimulate hair follicles and promote growth. The doctor will draw a small amount of blood, process it to concentrate the platelets and inject the PRP into your scalp. The procedure takes about 30 minutes and it’s market retail price in the UK averages at £500 per dose.
