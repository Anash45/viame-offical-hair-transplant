Getting Here

We want every stage of your visit to be as smooth and stress-free as possible. Whether you’re travelling from within London or arriving from outside the city, you’ll find our Harley Street clinic conveniently located and well connected.

Nearest Underground Stations

  • Regent’s Park (Bakerloo line) – approximately 4 minutes’ walk
  • Oxford Circus (Victoria, Central & Bakerloo lines) – around 7 minutes’ walk
  • Bond Street (Elizabeth & Central lines) – around 10 minutes’ walk

Travelling from Outside London

If you’re arriving by train, the nearest major stations with direct links to Harley Street are:

  • Marylebone Station – around 10 minutes by taxi or 15 minutes on foot
  • Euston Station – around 10 minutes by taxi
  • Paddington Station – around 12 minutes by taxi
  • King’s Cross St Pancras – around 15 minutes by taxi

Nearest Bus Stops

Bus routes serving the surrounding area include 18, 27, 30, 205, 453 and C2, with stops along Marylebone Road and Portland Place, both within a 3–5 minute walk from the clinic.

Parking

Please note that parking on Harley Street is limited to street-level, pay-and-display bays. We recommend allowing extra time to find a space or using one of the nearby public car parks such as
Q-Park Oxford Street or NCP Portland Place, both within a 5–10 minute walk.
